An Online application for Government departments to manage their court cases.

The Court Case Monitoring System(CCMS) software is the digital diary,which keeps everything in orgranised and easily accessible.

Our feature rich application Departments case Diary has all the value added features that a department always look foorward to having to meet his or her expectations to simplify court case tracking.
